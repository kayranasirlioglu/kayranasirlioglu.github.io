ABOUT ME

Hi there! I am a passionate audio engineer with a deep passion for everything related to music and sound. When I downloaded a DAW for the first time, I would have never thought that I would end up having 6+ years of experience in the industry, across 3 continents (also buying this much gear I don’t need was a surprise, if we are being honest)… After countless mixing sessions of many different genres such as jazz, classical, rock, EDM, and folk music, Nowadays, I spend most of my time with Game Audio and Mastering Engineering (while continuing to buy equipment I don’t need).

Throughout my career, I produced and recorded various (SAYIYI YAZ, DUSUR + EKLE) songs and SFXs for games and short films. I provide mixing, mastering, sound design, foley recording, and many different audio-based services for a wide variety of clients: ranging from rock bands who want to record their first EP to high-profile orchestras; or from industry-leading gaming companies to groups competing in game jams.

I still keep on honing my skills in recording, mixing, and mastering; always striving to deliver sonic experiences of the highest quality. Whether it's capturing the energy of a live performance or crafting the perfect mix in the studio, I am dedicated to bringing out the best in every sound. I thrive on collaborating with artists and producers, understanding their vision, and translating it into captivating audio. You are more than welcome to join me on this sonic journey, in which we can build immersive and unforgettable experiences together.
