ABOUT ME

Hi there! I am a passionate audio engineer with a deep love for music and sound. With years of experience in the industry, I have honed my skills in recording, mixing, and mastering; always striving to deliver the highest quality sonic experiences. Whether it's capturing the energy of a live performance or crafting the perfect mix in the studio, I am dedicated to bringing out the best in every sound. I thrive on collaborating with artists and producers, understanding their vision, and translating it into captivating audio. Join me on this sonic journey as we create immersive and unforgettable experiences together.

I give mixing and mastering, sound designing, foley recording and many different audio-based services for others ranging from start-up bands to high-profile artists, from industury-standart gaming companies to game jam groups.

Throughout my career, I produced and recorded many songs and SFXs for games and short films. In addition, I worked on many mixing sessions in various genres such as jazz, classical, rock, EDM, folk and many others. I focused on Game Audio and Mastering Engineering these days.
